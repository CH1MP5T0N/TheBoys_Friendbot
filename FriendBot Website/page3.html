    Main Talk to FriendBot Find a Friend

Tired of talking to FriendBot? You can try your luck on talking to other real strangers! Think of it like omegle but with a few rules and guidelines! What Find a Friend does is pick a stranger that you can chat with. You can turn on your camera if you feel comfortable and we are NOT responsible for the spreading of addresses, passwords and other personal details. We also have rules and guidelines to prohibit some words that could encourage self-harm and have a system to show people who are trusted.
